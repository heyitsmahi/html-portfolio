This was my very first kdrama which was recommended by my friend during covid and it will always be my one of the all time favourite kdrama. Sonce I like romantic dramas mainly so I liked this very much. this is story about four famous young boys and a poor girl who got messed up with the boys but finally found her soulmate after a lot of struggles in which the main character's mother took a very big part(for troubleshooting them with problems). But all those struggles was worth it.
